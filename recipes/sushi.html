Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish consisting of small balls or rolls of vinegar-flavored cold cooked rice
served with a garnish of raw fish, vegetables, or egg.

Ingredients
  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Fresh fish (e.g., tuna, salmon)
  • Vegetables (e.g., cucumber, avocado)
  • Wasabi and soy sauce (for serving)
  1. Cook Rice: Cook sushi rice and season with vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Prepare Fillings: Slice fish and vegetables into thin strips.
  3. Roll Sushi: Place nori sheet on bamboo mat, spread rice, add fillings, and roll tightly.
  4. Cut Rolls: Slice rolls into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Serve: Arrange on plate with wasabi and soy sauce.
